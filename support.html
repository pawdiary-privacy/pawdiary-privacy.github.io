Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data backed up anywhere?
No — PawDiary stores everything locally on your device only. If you delete the app or lose your device, your entries won't be recoverable. Be mindful of this when offloading or resetting your phone.

Does PawDiary require an internet connection?
No, PawDiary works fully offline.

How do I delete all my data?
Open Settings within the app for the option to clear all data, or simply delete the app from your device.
