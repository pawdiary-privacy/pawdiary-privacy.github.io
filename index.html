PawDiary Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

PawDiary ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data the App handles.

Data Collection

PawDiary does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, and makes no network requests.

Data Storage

All information you enter into PawDiary — including your dog's profile, diary entries, photos, and vaccination records — is stored locally on your device only, using standard on-device storage. This data is never uploaded to any server and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Photos and Camera

PawDiary requests access to your camera and photo library solely so you can attach photos to diary entries. These photos are saved to your device's local app storage and are never transmitted anywhere.

Third Parties

PawDiary does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time from the Settings screen within the App, or by deleting the App from your device.

Children's Privacy

PawDiary does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised "last updated" date.
